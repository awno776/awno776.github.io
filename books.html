Books

Here are some book recommendations to expand your mind during this downtime. Enjoy!

The Defining Decade

This book has completely altered the way I view the world around me, and reminded me of the importance of being intentional in every decision. Meg Jay provides real-world examples and data to help us all make the optimal decisions through the early years of life; she helps us avoid waiting until its too late to start creating the life we want. Highly recommended!

The Laws of Human Nature

Robert Greene beautifully describes the different laws of human behavior, and allows us to learn skills to navigate communication with ourselves and others by openly analyzing and studying the people around us.

Learn More

The Four Agreements

This book gives us four simple rules to follow when navigating through life: be impeccable with your word, never assume, don't take anything personally, and try your very best. Definitely a book that you'll want to read a few times over; this has dramatically changed the way I interact with the world around me. This book will allow you to improve your life by controlling your mindset and the way you interact with the world around you.

Learn More

All About Love: New Visions

An inside look on the way our love language is defined by childhood, by genetics, by emotions, etc. Really helps you learn about your inner workings and analyze your relationships fully.

Learn More

You'll Grow Out of It

An incredibly funny yet vulnerable piece about this comedian's life, trial and error, dating, and so much more. Incredibly funny, yet refreshingly candid. Will make you laugh and cry.

Learn More

Bella Figura

This book tells the story of a woman who wanted to start over. She learned the ways of Italian life, and in the process rediscovered a life that she thought she'd lost. Wonderfully unplifting and deeply relatable.
