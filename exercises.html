Meditations

App How to Make the Most of It Comments

Headspace

Each morning and night, I meditate using the Headspace App. This app guides you through easy meditations and allows you to learn the basics of meditation. It includes programs to reach any specific goals that you may have. This has been a godsend for me; connecting with my inner being has allowed me to better accept and love the people and the world around me.

Love Meditation

I have referred so many people to this app, and it has brought me so much joy to watch my loved ones work on themselves and find more peace using this technique.

Workouts

Exercise How to Make the Most of It Comments

Running

Getting adequate aerobic exercise is so important, and with all the gyms closed its not as easy as it's always been to get your heart pumping. Running around your neighborhood (preferably alone for social distancing purposes) allows us to get the exercise we need without the equipment of a gym.

To help make the time fly, Apple Music has all the variey of music you could ever desire.

Yoga

Yoga helps you work out your mind and body all at once; getting in-tune with our inner and outer selves is so important, especially at a time like this. Yoga is a great activity to help us grow.

Youtube has some wonderful begginer's yoga videos to choose from.
